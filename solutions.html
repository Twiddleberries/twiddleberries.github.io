Problem: Water Wastage

Based on a 2022 survey done on 13 year olds, it was shown that more than 50% of respondants showered for more than 20 minutes.

This is rather concerning since the amount of water used could be put to use elsewhere.

Therefore, we decided that Water Wastage would be a problem we want to work on.
